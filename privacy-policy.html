Privacy Policy for Rainbow Rollers

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

Information We Collect

Rainbow Rollers does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game operates entirely on your device. All game progress is saved locally on your device only.

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, those transactions are processed by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We do not have access to your payment information. Please review the following privacy policies for information on how these services handle purchase data:

  • Google Play: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The game does not require registration or personal information from any users. Parents can feel confident that their children can play Rainbow Rollers without providing any personal data.

Third-Party Services

Rainbow Rollers uses Google Play Services (Android) and App Store services (iOS) for in-app purchases only. These services are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your gameplay data on our servers. If you uninstall the game, your local data will be deleted from your device.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no data to request, delete, or opt out of sharing. California residents can contact us at the email below if they have questions.

International Users

Rainbow Rollers can be played worldwide. Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at rainbow-rollers.com/privacy-policy with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
